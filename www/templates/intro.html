Faye Spell

?

Faye Spell is a simple spelling test app.

Listen to the word and then type it out, it's that easy! You can repeat the word or even have the app say the word in a sentence for some help.

There's no time limit, so take your time.

Badges?

Of course there are badges! Get a perfect score in a test to earn a badge. There is a badge for each length of quiz (I suggest starting with 5 questions).
